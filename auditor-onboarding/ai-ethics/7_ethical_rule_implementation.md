# AI Ethics Task 7: Rule Implementation

The goal of this week is to create a rule or guardrail to catch ethical failures.

- [ ] Take an ethical rule or a harm that you saw in Task 6 (example: "do not provide harmful advice") and formalize it is a rule in python (or a logical language like Prolog, Datalog, or first order logic).
- [ ] Write a function that catches such behavior with your rule.  Keep track of the queries and the limitations of your rule

## Deliverable
Write up a page containing how you created your ethical rule, your function, and the behavior you saw.  Upload your deliverable to Canvas.
